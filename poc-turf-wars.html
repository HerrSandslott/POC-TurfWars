How to win
Eiminate all opponents or control more than half the centers.

How to move
Click glowing tiles to send your thugs to claim turf and grow your gang.

Moves per turn: 1
+1 if you control >1 street
+1 per controlled center

How to control a street
Own all 3 slots on any edge of a block.

How to gain access to a center
Control all 8 surrounding slots of that block.

Movement within a block
You may move to any horizontal or vertical adjacent slot, or to any surrounding slot if you control the center.

How to move to an orthogonally adjacent block
Control the street on your side to move

How to move to a diagonally adjacent block
Control both streets on your side to move from corner to corner
